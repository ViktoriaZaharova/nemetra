Hi, I am Nemetra…

And who are YOU when no one is watching?

Who?

Author, Writer, Poet, Artist, Philosopher, Seeker...

For me, writing is like music – every page, every sentence, every word should play in unison. I believe in the musical artistry of poetic ambition. I think that lyrical beauty is what defines a great storyteller.

Nemetra

Life Is A Game, Right?

So let’s play it! All of us. Following the rules, and sometimes breaking them too. Raising high with our blissful emotions and falling into despair sometimes. We are not alone. Because every feeling is a song. And every song has a right to be heard and appreciated by those who dare to listen with their hearts, not just with their ears.

I love to write about all kinds of emotions. And often, through my writing, I tune to the truth of our existence when I can hear the songs of my soul, find the answers and realise the beauty of living. I am keen to share my thoughts and feelings on my blog with the humbled hope of inspiring the seekers like me.

This blog is for YOU,
my dear readers, thinkers and wonderers.
